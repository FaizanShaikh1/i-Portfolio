Pizza Sales Report with Tableau and MySQL

In my quest to combine my love for pizza with my passion for data analytics, I took on the challenge of creating a comprehensive Pizza Sales Report using Tableau and MySQL.

Highlights:

  • Data Source: I collected sales data from multiple pizza outlets using MySQL, ensuring a clean and reliable dataset.
  • Powerful Visualizations: Tableau's intuitive interface allowed me to create stunning visualizations that showcase key performance metrics, such as sales trends, top-selling pizzas, and Best-selling pizzas.
  • Data Transformation: I performed data cleansing, transformation, and normalization to ensure accuracy and consistency in the report.
  • Interactive Dashboard: The final report includes an interactive dashboard that enables users to explore sales data, filter by date, location, and pizza type, and gain actionable insights.
  • Business Impact: By analyzing this data, pizza shop owners can make informed decisions, optimize their menu offerings, and enhance customer satisfaction.

What’s Inside the Report:

On the Home Page

  1. KPI’s: - There are 5 KPI’s Which shows Total Revenue, Average Order Value, Total Pizza Sold, Total Orders and Average Pizzas Per Order.
  2. Column Chart: - I use Column Chart to get the Daily Trend for Total Order.
  3. Area Chart: - I use Area Chart to get the Monthly Trend for Total orders.
  4. Funnel: - I use Funnel to get Total Pizza Sold by Pizza Category.

On the Best/Worst Sellers Page

There are 6 Bar Charts which show:

  1. Top 5 Pizzas by Revenue
  2. Bottom 5 Pizzas by Revenue
  3. Top 5 Pizzas by Quantity
  4. Bottom 5 Pizzas by Quantity
  5. Top 5 Pizzas by Orders
  6. Bottom 5 Pizzas by Orders

There is a page navigator button that helps us switch between two pages (HOME AND BEST/WORST SELLERS). Two Slicers are also available to filter out insights based on pizza category and order date.

This Dashboard helped me understand how the MySQL server management studio works and how we can connect it with Tableau to build beautiful and attractive visuals.
