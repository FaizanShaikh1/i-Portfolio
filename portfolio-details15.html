Tableau Dashboard: Amazon Sales Report 2022 in India

I'm excited to share my recent Tableau dashboard on Amazon Sales Report of 2022 in India. The idea behind this project is to explore the previous year's (2022) Amazon Sales Report and get the overall visualization of sales in the form of a dashboard. This visualization will generate key insights that will help the sales team of Amazon make informed decisions to fine-tune their sales strategy.

Key Insights from the Dataset:

  1. Quantity By Week and Category: Monitors the total quantities of products based on the weeks and categories of the product and presents it through a bar chart.

  2. Quantity By Courier Status and Category: Analyzes the total quantities based on the courier status (Shipped, Unshipped, Cancelled) and categories through a pie chart.

  3. Quantity By Sales Channel and Category & B2B Sales Quantity: Gets the quantities based on sales channel, category, and B2B Sales and presents it through a square box.

  4. Amount By Week and Category: Monitors total amount based on the weeks and categories through a line chart.

  5. Quantity By Status and Category: Analyzes total quantities by status (shipped, pending, shipped-delivery to the buyer, etc.) and all categories through a bar chart.

  6. Top 10 States By Quantity and Category - Ship-Service-Level: Identify the top 10 states on the basis of total quantities, categories, and ship-service-level (Expedited, Standard).

  7. Quantity By State: Get insights into how the total quantities of products are distributed in the states of the country using the map.

  8. Amount By Size and Category: Analyzes the total amounts based on the size of the products and categories.

  9. Quantity By Size and Category: Monitors the total quantities based on the size of the products and categories.
