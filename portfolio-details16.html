Approach to Web Marketing Analysis

In the realm of web marketing, a methodical approach is indispensable for optimizing performance and achieving success. The journey towards an improved web presence and enhanced conversion rates necessitates a systematic strategy that encompasses the following key stages:

Data Understanding: The foundation of effective web marketing analysis begins with a comprehensive grasp of the available data. This stage involves gaining insights into the metrics and indicators that shape the portrait of your web performance.

Data Cleansing: With the data in hand, the next critical step is data cleansing. This process involves meticulous data integrity checks, correction of inconsistencies, and elimination of inaccuracies.

Data Analysis: Once the data is in its pristine form, the focus shifts to data analysis. During this stage, the collected data is processed, and trends, patterns, and anomalies are identified.

Data Insights: The culmination of this analytical journey leads to the extraction of invaluable data insights. These insights serve as the guiding star for making strategic decisions in web marketing.

Developing the Dashboard

The creation of our comprehensive web marketing dashboard adhered to a structured methodology, encompassing the following steps:

1. Data Extraction: The initial step involved the extraction of data from multiple sources, gathering the raw material for analysis.

2. Data Transformation: Following data extraction, transformations were applied to shape the data into a format conducive to analysis.

3. Data Loading: The refined data was then loaded into the platform, laying the foundation for further exploration.

4. Data Modeling: In this stage, data models were meticulously constructed, establishing relationships between various data sets.

5. DAX Formulas: Data Analysis Expressions (DAX) were intricately designed to perform advanced calculations, adding depth to our analysis.

6. Report Construction: The core of the dashboard took shape as reports were meticulously crafted, presenting insights in a visually comprehensible manner.

7. Report Publishing: After the creation of reports, they were published to make them accessible and shareable.

8. Dashboard Creation: The final step culminated in the development of an interactive and informative dashboard, compacting key insights and metrics into a user-friendly interface.

Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

Our web marketing analysis dashboard encapsulates an array of critical KPIs, offering valuable insights into the performance and impact of your web marketing efforts. These include:

Total Sessions, Total Exits, Total Bounces, Average Time on Page, Sessions from the Previous Year, Sessions by Month, Unique Pageviews, Average Page Load Time, Total Sessions and Bounces, Top 5 Page Titles by Unique Pageviews, Top 5 Countries by Unique Pageviews, Cancellations by Hotel, Sessions by Device Category, Top 5 Sessions by Source Medium, Sessions by Channel Grouping, Total Revenue and Total Revenue Loss by Hotel, Top 5 Sessions by Country, Pageviews by Device Category, Top 5 Pageviews by Source Medium, Pageviews by Month, Top 5 Pageviews by Page Title, Page Load Time by Device Category, Page Load Time by Source Medium, Top 5 Page Titles by Average Page Load Time.
