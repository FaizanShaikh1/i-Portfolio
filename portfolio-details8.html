HR Analytics Dashboard using Power BI

This dashboard is designed to provide valuable insights into HR datasets, helping decision-makers plan their organizational strategy effectively. Below, I'll give you an overview of the topics covered in this Power BI project and the visualizations included in the dashboard.

Use Power BI: This Dashboard will walk you through the Power BI interface and its various components, ensuring you have a strong foundation in navigating and utilizing this powerful tool. I have learned about its intuitive features and functions within Power BI Desktop.

Importing Data in Power BI: I have dived into data importing in Power BI, covering how to import data from diverse sources like Excel workbooks, CSV files, and databases. This knowledge will enable me to effectively bring data into Power BI for analysis.

Power Query in Power BI: I have gained a deep understanding of Power Query in Power BI and how to transform the data. I have explored functions such as data cleaning, adding columns, changing data types, and replacing values, ensuring your data is prepared for analysis.

Creating Charts in Power BI: I have utilized the wide range of charts and graphs available in Power BI to visualize the data effectively. I have learned how to craft the data into visual forms that convey insights clearly.

Filters and Slicers in Power BI: Filters and slicers are essential for interactively exploring your data and gaining deeper insights. I have covered how to use these features effectively to refine the data analysis.

Designing Dashboards in Power BI: I have learned how to design interactive dashboards that provide a comprehensive view of the data, making it easier for the audience to understand and interact with the insights.

About the Visualization:

Insights from the Dashboard: I have extracted actionable insights from the Power BI Dashboard. I have discovered techniques for identifying the number of employees available, attrition rates and numbers, the average age of employees, the gender distribution, satisfaction levels in different departments, and more.
